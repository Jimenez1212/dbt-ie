## Certification Exam Overview

- **Format**: 65 Multiple Choice Questions.
- **Time**: 2 Hours.
- **Passing**: 65%.
- **Focus**: Practical application, not just theory.

## Best Practices Checklist

- [ ] **Naming**: Consistent `snake_case`.
- [ ] **Structure**: Staging -> Intermediate -> Marts.
- [ ] **Testing**: Test all Primary Keys and Foreign Keys.
- [ ] **Documentation**: Descriptions for all public models.
- [ ] **Git**: Atomic commits, descriptive PRs.
